Buoy-Watch: AI-Powered Rip Current Early Warning System

Location: Parangtritis Beach, Yogyakarta

Project Overview

Buoy-Watch is an innovative Early Warning System (EWS) designed to detect and alert beachgoers of dangerous rip currents in real-time. Developed for the PKM-KC program, this system integrates AI-based computer vision with floating smart buoys to provide proactive safety measures in high-risk coastal zones.

Intellectual Property

This system is an original innovation developed under the Student Creativity Program (PKM-KC) and is officially protected as a unique scientific innovation.
