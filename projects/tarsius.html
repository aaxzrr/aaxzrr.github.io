Tarsius: Earthquake early warning and real-time seismic monitoring system

Research Period: May 2025 â€“ December 2025

Project Overview

Tarsius is a software platform developed for automated Earthquake Detection and Real-time seismic monitoring. It offers a set of tools to identify, analyze, and process seismic events, with primary applications in early warning systems and seismic hazard mitigation. This research is crucial for understanding the volcanic and tectonic characteristics of the region.

Key Technical Roles

  • End-to-end Seismic Data Processing, from raw signal cleaning to inversion.
