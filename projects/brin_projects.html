Seismic Attenuation Tomography of the Sunda Strait

Research Period: March 2024 – November 2024

Project Overview

This project involves processing seismic data to identify attenuation anomalies in the Sunda Strait. Using methods like Spectral Fitting with custom Python scripts and exist software Simul2023, I analyzed the seismic wave behavior to map subsurface structures. This research is crucial for understanding the volcanic and tectonic characteristics of the region.

  • Advanced visualization of subsurface anomalies using GMT (Generic Mapping Tools).
